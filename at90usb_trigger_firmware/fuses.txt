fuses

From the AT90USB1287 manual, the defaults should be:
extended fuse: 0xF3
fuse high: 0x99
fuse low: 0x62
lock bit byte: 0xEC

From the write-gone-bad, they were (maybe)

fuse low:      0x5e -> 0x2a
fuse high:     0x99 -> 0x2a
extended fuse: 0xf3 -> 0x2a


now:

efuse: 0xf3
hfuse: 0x99
lfuse: 0x5e (normal for AT90USBKEY, I think)
lock: 0xFF (normal for AT90USBKEY, I think)

avrdude -p AT90USB1287 -c jtagmkII -P usb -U hfuse:r:-:r | xxd
